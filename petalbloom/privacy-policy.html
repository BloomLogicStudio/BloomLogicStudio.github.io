PetalBloom Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 17, 2026

PetalBloom is a casual puzzle game. We respect your privacy and designed the app to avoid collecting personal information.

Data Collection

PetalBloom does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share personal information from users.

Local Game Data

PetalBloom may store gameplay-related information locally on your device. This may include level progress, highest unlocked level, Gold balance, power-up inventory, daily reward status, sound settings, music settings, haptic preferences, and similar game preferences.

This information is used only to operate the game and improve your gameplay experience. This local game data is not transmitted to us.

In-App Purchases

PetalBloom may offer optional in-app purchases, such as Gold packs or other game-related items. In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive, collect, or store your payment card information or Apple account payment details.

Daily Rewards and Game Progress

PetalBloom may save daily reward status, reward claim history, current level, unlocked levels, and inventory counts locally on your device. This allows the game to remember your progress and rewards between play sessions.

Third-Party Services

PetalBloom does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising tracking, account login, cloud save, or crash reporting services.

If third-party services are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated to explain what data, if any, is collected or shared.

Children’s Privacy

PetalBloom does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child, we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Data Security

Since PetalBloom does not collect personal information, we do not maintain personal user data on external servers. Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

PetalBloom Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2026

PetalBloom is a casual flower garden puzzle game. We respect your privacy and designed the app to avoid requiring accounts, logins, or personal profile information.

Data Collection

PetalBloom does not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, phone number, or other personal profile information to play the game.

PetalBloom stores gameplay information locally on your device. PetalBloom also uses Google AdMob to provide optional rewarded advertisements. Google AdMob may collect or receive certain information from your device to provide, measure, and improve advertising.

Local Game Data

PetalBloom may store gameplay-related information locally on your device. This may include level progress, highest unlocked level, Gold balance, booster inventory, daily reward status, sound settings, music settings, haptic preferences, tutorial status, and similar game preferences.

This information is used to operate the game and improve your gameplay experience. This local game data is stored on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Advertising and Rewarded Ads

PetalBloom uses Google AdMob to provide optional rewarded advertisements. Players may choose to watch an advertisement to earn in-game rewards, such as Garden Gold.

Rewarded ads are optional. You may continue playing PetalBloom without choosing to watch rewarded ads.

Google AdMob may collect or receive certain information from your device to provide, measure, and improve advertising. This may include device identifiers, approximate location derived from IP address, app activity, ad interactions, diagnostics, and related usage data, depending on your device settings, region, and consent choices.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can learn more about Google’s privacy practices here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

PetalBloom may offer optional in-app purchases, such as Gold packs or other game-related items. In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive, collect, or store your payment card information or Apple account payment details.

Daily Rewards and Game Progress

PetalBloom may save daily reward status, reward claim history, current level, unlocked levels, Gold balance, and inventory counts locally on your device. This allows the game to remember your progress and rewards between play sessions.

Third-Party Services

PetalBloom uses Google AdMob for optional rewarded advertisements.

PetalBloom does not currently use account login, cloud save, or third-party account profile services.

If additional third-party services are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated to explain what data, if any, is collected or shared.

Children’s Privacy

PetalBloom does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child, we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Rewarded advertisements may be provided by Google AdMob. Parents and guardians should review device, App Store, and advertising privacy settings as appropriate.

Data Security

PetalBloom does not maintain personal user accounts or personal profile information on external servers. Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Data collected or processed by Google AdMob is handled according to Google’s privacy practices and policies.

Your Choices

You can choose not to watch rewarded advertisements. You may also adjust privacy and advertising settings through your device settings.

You can delete PetalBloom from your device to remove locally stored game progress and preferences from that device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
